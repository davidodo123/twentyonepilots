ABOUT US

Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio, formed in 2009 by Tyler Joseph along with former members Nick Thomas and Chris Salih. After releasing their self-titled debut album that same year, the group began building a small but loyal following in their hometown.

Their second self-released record, Regional at Best (2011), revealed the band’s developing style — a distinctive mix of alternative rock, pop, hip-hop, and electronic elements combined with deeply personal lyrics. Shortly after that release, Thomas and Salih left the band, and Joseph continued the project with drummer Josh Dun, forming the lineup that would soon bring the duo international attention.

In 2012, their energetic live performances and unique sound attracted the interest of Fueled by Ramen, a subsidiary of Atlantic Records, leading to their first major-label deal. The following year they released *Vessel*, which included songs like “Holding on to You,” “Car Radio,” and “House of Gold.” The album explored anxiety, faith, and self-doubt through an energetic and experimental sound that resonated with a growing audience.
